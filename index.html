Engaging minds outside the classroom.

At Excellence Academy, we believe education extends far beyond the four walls of a classroom. Our diverse range of extracurricular activities is designed to foster creativity, teamwork, and leadership skills in every student.

Featured Extracurricular

Building character, leadership, and survival skills.

Scout Program

Excellence Academy Scouts

Our flagship extracurricular program is designed to take students out of their comfort zones and into the great outdoors. The Excellence Academy Scout troop is one of the highest-rated in the region.

Through weekend camping trips, community service projects, and rigorous outdoor training, students learn invaluable life lessons that cannot be taught inside a traditional classroom.

  • 🏕️ Survival Skills: Navigation, wilderness first aid, and fire-building.
  • 🤝 Teamwork: Overcoming physical and mental challenges together.
  • 🏅 Leadership: Earning merit badges and leading junior troops.
  • 🌍 Community: Regular environmental cleanups and local service.
